### Perceptual Hashing (PHash, DHash, WHash)

Unlike simple MD5 hash comparison, perceptual hashing analyzes the actual visual content of images. This allows detection of:
- Images that have been resized
- Photos that have been compressed
- Pictures with minor edits
- Rotated or flipped versions
- Images with slight color adjustments

Our Convolutional Neural Network model (powered by PyTorch) provides the most accurate similar image detection:
- Detects cropped versions of images
- Finds photos with different filters applied
- Identifies images after significant editing
- Recognizes same subject from different angles
- Works across different lighting conditions
